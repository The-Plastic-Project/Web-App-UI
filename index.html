About us

The Plastic Project is a student led project started near Seattle, Washington. We aim to tackle pollution and promote an eco-friendly lifestyle by equipping communities with resources through our progressive web application. This app helps people learn about local eco-friendly businesses they can get involved with and contains technology-driven tools to help people live more sustainably. t.
